Intro

After starting my career as a trader in Chicago, I realized I wanted my work to have a greater impact. Leveraging the tech skills I built in trading, I pivoted to the tech industry.

In early 2023, I was diagnosed with a cancerous tumor that required immediate surgery. Navigating the broken U.S. healthcare system—facing denied claims, endless paperwork, and bureaucracy—was eye-opening. That experience fueled my drive to contribute to meaningful change in healthcare.

Now, I’m exploring opportunities where I can apply my analytical skills and problem-solving mindset to industries that need real change. I'm especially drawn to healthcare and tech, where smarter systems and better solutions can directly improve lives. More than anything, I want to work on problems worth solving.
