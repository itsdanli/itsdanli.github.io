Intro

After earning my degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, I launched my career in the fast-paced and high-pressure world of trading in Chicago. Over four years in this dynamic industry, I honed advanced analytical skills and gained a deep understanding of market dynamics. Success in trading demanded persistence, adaptability, and a sharp ability to learn on the fly.

During this time, I developed technical expertise in various programming languages and statistical evaluation methods to analyze and test trading strategies. I also designed tools for automation and created intuitive dashboards to visualize data. These experiences cultivated my passion for problem-solving and building innovative solutions—a passion that has shaped my professional journey ever since.

After a period of reflection, I made the decision to relocate to Denver for a fresh start. While I continue to hold a strong interest in financial markets, I’ve focused on broadening my skill set to address challenges across industries. With companies increasingly reliant on cloud infrastructure, I am working to deepen my expertise in architecting scalable and efficient cloud-based solutions.

This personal blog is my space to document my growth and share insights into the projects I’m working on. Whether you’ve found me through LinkedIn or my resume, I appreciate you taking the time to learn more about me.
